Design philosophy

Considering people in context—who they are, what they care about, and what they want to do—is essential to effective design.

I'm committed to using technology, design, and simple writing to empower people to accomplish good things. You'll never hear me refer to people as "users" because I'm always thinking about them beyond their interactions with an interface.

As an information professional, my job is to discover people's needs (stated, observed, anticipated) and understand the larger contexts in which they live, play, and work. Investing first and foremost in empathizing with people and their goals allows me to create solutions that accommodate people, rather than asking people to accommodate solutions.
